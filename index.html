About Me

Hello, I'm Geri! I'm a 21-year-old college student currently in my third year at Pangasinan State University, where I'm pursuing my passion for computer engineering. I call the picturesque town of Rosario in Pozorrubio, Pangasinan, my hometown, which has instilled in me a deep appreciation for nature and the simple joys of life. During my academic journey, I've had the opportunity to explore the exciting worlds of Python and Java, thanks to my coursework. My goal is to further develop my programming skills and eventually contribute to innovative tech solutions that can make a positive impact on our society.

Facial Recognition System Using Arduino

The "Facial Recognition Security System with Arduino and Python" project is designed to provide robust home security by utilizing advanced facial recognition technology. Its main objective is to allow authorized individuals seamless access to their homes based on their facial features. The system captures and analyzes facial data using Python and Arduino.
